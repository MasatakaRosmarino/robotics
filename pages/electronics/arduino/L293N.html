Experiment :

Determine output voltage

sketch
                                
                                    int motor1     = 8; 
                                    int direction1 = 9; 
                                        
                                    void setup(){ 
                                        pinMode(motor1, OUTPUT);  
                                        pinMode(direction1, OUTPUT);  
                                    } 
                                    
                                    void loop(){ 
                                        digitalWrite(motor1, HIGH);   
                                        digitalWrite(direction1, LOW);             
                                    }

Equipment needed

  • 1.5 V AA batteries x 6
  • 6 AA battery holder
  • 4 AA battery holder
  • DC motor 130 size
  • Johnson 32903 DC motor
  • Multimeter
  • jumper wires

Observations with type 130 DC motor

  • Measured batteries voltage in the 6 AA battery pack: 9.37 V
  • Measured output voltage from L293N (motor 1): 4.03 V
  • With another L293N shield the output voltage is ~ 3.7 V

Observations with 6V input voltage

  • Measured batteries voltage in the 4 AA battery pack: 5.9 V (partally discharged)
  • Measured output voltage from L293N (motor 1): 366 mV

Observations with Johnson 32903 DC motor

  • Measured batteries voltage in the 6 AA battery pack: 9.22 V
  • Measured output voltage from L293N (motor 1): 2.9 - 3 V

Experiment :

Equipment needed

Same equipment as that used in the previous experiment.

Connections

  • L293N ENA pin to Arduino pin 13
  • L293N input1 pin to Arduino pin 12
  • L293N input2 pin to Arduino pin 11
sketch
                                
                                    void setup() {
                                        pinMode (13, OUTPUT);
                                        pinMode (12, OUTPUT);
                                        pinMode (11, OUTPUT);
                                      }
                                      
                                      void loop() {
                                        digitalWrite (12, HIGH);
                                        digitalWrite (11, LOW);
                                        digitalWrite (13, HIGH);
                                        delay (10000);
                                        digitalWrite (13, LOW);
                                        delay (5000);
                                        digitalWrite (12, HIGH);
                                        digitalWrite (11, LOW);
                                        digitalWrite (13, HIGH);
                                        delay (10000);
                                        digitalWrite (13, LOW);
                                        delay (5000); 
                                      }

Observations with 9 V supply and Johnson DC motor

  • Voltage measured from battery pack: 9.2 V
  • Voltage measured in johnson motor: 4.03 - 6.29 V
Second measurement
  • Voltage measured from battery pack: 8.72 V
  • Voltage measured in johnson motor: 5.4 V

Observation with 6 V supply and type 130 DC motor

  • Voltage measured from battery pack: 5.89 V
  • Voltage measured in motor: 4.4 V
