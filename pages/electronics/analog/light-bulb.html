light bulb

Experiment 1:

Equipment needed

  • 1.5V AA battery
  • Single AA battery holder, or simply adhesive tape
  • Jumper cables X2
  • 1.5V light bulb
  • Digital multimeter
  • alligator clips
Setup
  1. connect the jumper cables to the 1.5V AA battery using the tape
  2. touch the light bulb to the other ends of the cables
    note: it doesn't matter which edge you touch

Procedure

measure the following values:

  • battery voltage
  • light bulb voltage
  • current through the circuit

note: Measurements show slightly different values from the ones declared.
