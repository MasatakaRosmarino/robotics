Experiment 1:

Equipment needed

  • 1.5V AA battery
  • Single AA battery holder, or simply adhesive tape
  • Jumper cables X3
  • 1.5V light bulb
  • Digital multimeter
  • Breadboard
Setup

Procedure

Before placing the components on the Breadboard, measure:

  • voltage across the battery
  • resistance across the diode
  • resistance across the light bulb

Once placed all the components on the Breadboard, measure:

  • voltage across the battery
  • voltage across the diode
  • voltage across the light bulb
  • current through the circuit

knowing diodes working

reverse the diode on the breadboard and see how the light bulb won't light up.
