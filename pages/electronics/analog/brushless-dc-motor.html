brushless dc toy motors

common commercial motor

The typical commercial model is the 130 size dc toy motor. Its features are the following:

  • Operating Temperature: -10°C ~ +60°C
  • Operating Voltage: 1.5 ~ 6.0VDC
  • Rated Load: 10 g*cm
  • No-load Current: 70 mA max
  • No-load Speed: 9100 ±1800 rpm
  • Loaded Current: 250 mA max
  • Loaded Speed: 4500 ±1500 rpm
  • Starting Torque: 20 g*cm
  • Stall Current: 500mA max
  • Body Size: 27.5mm x 20mm x 15mm
  • Shaft Size: 8mm x 2mm diameter
  • Weight: 15 ~ 17.5 grams

Observation
  1. AA battery voltage is 1.538V
  2. connecting 1 AA battery, the DC motor won't activate

with 2 AA 1.2 V rechargeable batteries in series

  • voltage across the batteries is 2.45 V
  • voltage across the motor is around 1.2 and up to 1.4 V
  • current through the circuit is between 0.44 and 0.46 A

with 4 AA rechargeable batteries having 1.2V

  • measured voltage is 5.024V
  • current goes from 0.8 to up to 1A
  • smoke detected

with 1 X 10 Ω resistor in series with the motor

  • motor is not spinning
  • 4 AA battery pack voltage is around 4.89 V
  • voltage across the resistor is around 3.7 V
  • voltage across the DC motor is less than 1 V, around 0.29 V
  • current through the circuit is around 0.38 A

NOTE: according to the motor datasheet, the operating voltage should be around 0.35 A.
putting a 10 Ω resistor in series should give this result, however, the resistor seems to be taking most of the voltage required to spin the motor.

with 1 X 10 Ω resistor in parallel with the motor

  • motor spinning fast and loudly
  • 4 AA battery pack voltage is around 4.89 V
  • voltage across the motor is around 3.5 V
  • current through the circuit goes up to 0.8 A

NOTE: adding up to 3 resistors in parallel with the motor won't change the amount of current through the latter, it stays between 0.7 and 0.8 A.

with 4 X 1 Ω resistors in series with the motor

  • 4 AA battery pack voltage is around 4.89 V
  • voltage across the 4 resistors is around 2.7 V
  • voltage across the DC motor is around 1.3 V
  • current through the circuit is around 0.5 A
