LEDs

Experiment 1:

Equipment needed

  • Resistors
    • 470 Ω, 1/4W
    • 1K Ω, 1/4W
    • 10K Ω, 1/4W
  • Jumper cables X 4
  • red LED (3mm or 5mm)
  • 9V battery
  • 9V battery connector
  • Breadboard
  • digital multimeter
Setup

note: the longer pin of the LED must connect to the positive pole of the battery. If the connection is reversed, it won't light up.

procedure

Before placing the components on the Breadboard, measure:

  • voltage across the battery
  • resistance across the 470 Ω resistor
  • resistance across the LED

Once placed all the components on the Breadboard, measure:

  • voltage across the 470 Ω resistor
  • voltage across the LED
  • current through the circuit
