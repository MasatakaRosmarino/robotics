Experiment 1:

Equipment needed

  • Jumper cables X 2
  • red LED (3mm or 5mm)
  • AAA battery
  • AA battery
  • button cell batteries:
    • CR2032
    • CR2016
    • LR44
  • C battery
  • D battery

note: the longer pin of the LED must connect to the positive pole of the battery. If the connection is reversed, it won't light up.

procedure

Before placing the components on the Breadboard, measure:

  • voltage across the battery
  • resistance across the LED
