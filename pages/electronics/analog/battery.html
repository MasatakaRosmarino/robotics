batteries

battery capacity and current rating chart:

batteries capacities are distinguished by mAh

  • aaa: 1,000 mAh
  • aa: 2400 mAh
  • c: 6000 mAh
  • d: 13000 mAh
  • n: 650 mAh
  • 9V (pp3): 500 mAh
Type Capacity (mAh) Current rating (mA)
aaa 1,000 10
aa 2,400 50
c 6,000 100
d 13000 200
n 650 10
9v (pp3) 500 15

this will most likely damage the battery due to its physical and chemical structure that limits the increase in current output.

batteries in series and in parallel

batteries in series

  • they increase their output voltage
  • the current released by them is the same as of a single battery

batteries in parallel

  • they increase their output current
  • the voltage released by them is the same as of a single battery

batteries in series and parallel

they can increase both output voltage and current.

IMPORTANT:

  • the type of the battery (AA, AAA, C, 9V etc.) must be the same
  • they need the same amount of charge as possible
