torque

Definition

Torque is referred to as the force applied at a certain distance from the fulchrum.

The vector representation is the following:

Given a force f applied at a distance d, the mathematical relationship is the following:

τ = F · d

If the distance is at a certain angle, the vector representation is the following:

The mathematical relationship is the following:

With respect to the angle θ:

τ = F · d · cos θ

With respect to the angle γ:

τ = F · d · sin γ

The common unit of measure is N · m or Kg · cm.

note: although it is the product of a force and distance, it does not represent the energy in a system.
